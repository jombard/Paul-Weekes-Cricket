Buy Better, Play Better

Paul Weekes launches a new range of bats and kit

Paul Weekes, former Middlesex CCC & England A (1988 - 2006) player is launching his own cricket bat range for young cricketers. Paul has over 25 years of coaching expertise both in the UK and world-wide, and has seen many colts playing with the wrong bats - too big, too small or too heavy.

Young players themselves often pick the wrong bat purely for the brand or stickers, without consideration for the quality and whether the bat is right for them. Having the wrong bat makes batting progression much more difficult, and colts can quickly become disheartened.

Having the correct equipment really does enable colts to perfect their technique.

With Paul's extended playing and coaching experience, his new range of Weekesy 100 bats will be selected personally by Paul and will be specifically aimed at providing a top quality bat purpose built for colts, at a reasonable cost.

The Weekesy 100 range will allow the colts to enhance their hand speed through the ball and hit the ball much harder equally off front and back foot with out losing their body shape and will help to improve their technique.

Paul will personally help choose the right bat for each individual - buy better, play better!

Pads, gloves & cricket balls also available.
